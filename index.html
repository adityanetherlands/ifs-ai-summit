The Ground Is Shifting

AI-native startups aren’t adding features to existing software. They’re replacing entire functions with new architectures that make traditional SaaS look slow and bloated. Sales, Marketing, HR, Finance, IT. They’re all in the crosshairs. IFS has a window: our industries adopt deliberately, which gives us time to lead. But that window is closing. This event is about making sure every leader here feels the urgency and leaves with a plan.

Before You Arrive

Your Homework

Three tasks to ground the conversation in your reality. Come prepared – you'll get far more from the sessions.

Required

1. Map Your Function's AI Landscape

Which 3–5 AI-native startups could replace parts of what your team does today? What can they do that your current stack can't?

Where to start: Search "AI-native [your function]" on Google and Product Hunt. Check Crunchbase for recent funding. Look at Clay, Glean, 11x.ai, Aisera – then find the equivalents for your domain.

Required

2. The 10% Thought Experiment

10% of today's headcount. Same budget – the rest spent on AI tooling. What do you automate first? What 2–5 things must be true to get there by Jan 2027?

How to think about it: List your team's top 10 tasks by time spent. For each: "Could AI do 80% of this today?" Then ask what your people do with the freed-up capacity. That's the real value.

Heads Up

3. Hands-On AI Sessions

You'll use AI tools intensively on both Tuesday and Wednesday. No prior experience needed – but you'll get far more out of it if you've experimented beforehand.

Tools & access details to follow. Ensure you have local admin rights on your laptop. Check with IT if unsure.
